Our Mission

StudyLite is a Kenyan-made digital revision platform designed to help KCSE students revise faster and smarter. We provide mobile-friendly notes, practice quizzes, and worked examples to strengthen understanding and improve exam performance.

What We Offer

  • Concise subject notes aligned with the KCSE syllabus
  • Interactive quizzes with instant feedback
  • Affordable downloadable revision packs (PDFs)

Our Vision

To make high-quality academic revision content accessible to every student across Kenya and beyond, regardless of location or device type.
